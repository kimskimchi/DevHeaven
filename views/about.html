About

Founded by four students, the goal of the website is to let developers test their apps with other users. With a simple intuitive design, users are able to look for the apps they are interested in testing, and allow developers to be up to date with their app testers. As a platform for both testers and developers, it features optional incentives by developers, and creates an atmosphere of choice for developers and testers alike.

Help

The left sidebar is what allows you to navigate to different pages.

To go back to your main page, click on the DevHeaven icon

The Browse tab lets you search through a list of apps. If you are searching for a specific app, type the app name you are looking for in the search bar at the top of the page

The My Apps tab lets you look at apps and look at details about it. If you want to create a new app for people to see and test, there is a button labelled new app. Once you click it, you enter the required information, and press the submit button

The My Projects tab allows you to see what apps you are helping out with. It will display the app name, the author, and a short summary of what the app is about.

The My Profile tab allows you to review your profile and information. There is also a button to allow you to make changes to your information

If you want to search for a specific user, use the Search user tab, and type in there name. For each user, it will show some information about them.

The logout button is on the top right of the page which will take you back to the home screen
